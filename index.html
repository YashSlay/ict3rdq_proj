What is Health and Safety Authority (on Fire Prevention)?

Fire Prevention - a strategy & fire risk assessment may include detail and an entire consideration of issues. Issues may include arising from heat, oxygen, fuel. These serve as guides without any particular order to advice and educate people about fire risks and awareness.

Fire protection is an everyday action. The fire regulations in force must be implemented in factories, living areas, public places and transportation. Education on fire and regular fire drills are also major compliance issues.


4 Different Ways to Prevent the Unwanted Spread of Fire

Avoid Using Outlets/Plug-in Power Bars Excessively		 May cause an overload or overpower in the electrical circuit. Immediately have old, damaged outlets replaced or repaired.

Properly Extinguish Smoking Materials		 Do not neglect to extinguish smoking materials and turn-off open stoves once done cooking. Make sure flames and smoke residue is properly disposed of after usage.

Store Cooking Oil AWAY from Stoves		 Cooking oil is considered as a flammable liquid and may cause sudden ignition of flames when unattended. Be sure to keep it a safe distance away from stoves especially during the process of cooking.

Keep a Fire Extinguisher in Check		 When worse comes to worst, always know the various locations where your residence fire extinguishers are placed. It's a prime tool in need of extinguishing unexpected fire hazards.
